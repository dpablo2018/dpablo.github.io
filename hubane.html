Hubane

By: Daniel Pablo

Hubane is one of the most unique underground clothing brands coming out of New York. The founder is Israel Yanir who founded the clothing brand his Freshmen Year of College.
I first saw Hubane's unique pieces from a Youtuber(Tristan Paine) who walks around the streets of New York and asks people what they are wearing.
What really stood out to me was this frosty, master chief-type helmet on their website and instagram. Spikes are coming out and it just looks so sick.
Besides the awesome helmets, I was also drawn to their sunglasses which gave a Venom look from Spiderman. Most of their pieces have this look and are really well crafted.
